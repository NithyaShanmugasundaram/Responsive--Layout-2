About CPRO

The Communications & Public Relations Office (CPRO)is the multi-channel communications team of City University of Hong Kong. CPRO builds upon CityU's strengths to enhance the university's outreach and image-building efforts. A key objective of CPRO is to help the University meey new challenges by building trust and understanding among the university community and the public.
